§ 02

About the work.

A brief introduction.

I'm a chemist working at the intersection of nanomaterials synthesis and electrochemistry — building the next generation of point-of-care and wearable biosensors.

Across six peer-reviewed publications and one filed patent, I've developed novel MXene heterostructures with transition metal oxides, dichalcogenides, and metal-organic frameworks. These architectures form the transducing heart of devices that detect stress, sleep, and histamine biomarkers in real time — from syringe-integrated yarn electrodes to wearable patches.

My training spans five institutes: IIT Palakkad, NIT Calicut, NIT Andhra Pradesh, IIT Indore, and Fatima Mata National College. I'm currently seeking doctoral positions in electrochemistry, nanomaterials, or biosensing.
